# Code of Conduct

TheOpenHub Skills Studio follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful,
specific, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Focus critique on ideas, code, tests, and documentation.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give actionable feedback and accept reasonable disagreement.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when they affect technical decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Bad-faith security reports or social engineering attempts.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily limit, or permanently remove participants
from project spaces when behavior violates this code. Security-sensitive reports
may be moved to private handling according to `SECURITY.md`.

To report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers listed in `GOVERNANCE.md`.
